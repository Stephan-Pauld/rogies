About Me.

Born and raised in Kamloops B.C recently fell in love with Lethbridge, these gorgeous farms and amazing produce. My husband and I one day just packed up, left and couldnt be happier! We met in culinary school many years ago and share a passion for food! I have been making pierogies with my family all my life, every Christmas and thanks giving me and my sisters gather with my grandma in the kitchen and hand make pierogies old school for hours and hours! They are always a hit! we share the leftovers with friends and family since we always make enough for a small army <3
